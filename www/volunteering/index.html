Volunteering

Consider your origin: you were not formed to live like brutes but to follow virtue and knowledge. — Dante Alighieri (1306)

Thank you for your interest in helping the free software community, this platform is always in need of volunteers!

  • you must be at least 18 years old
  • you must be able to read and write in English
  • you should have 4 hours of free time every week

Volunteers may be tasked with:

  • approving or rejecting the edits suggested by visitors
  • evaluate and organize the technical feedback submitted by visitors
  • protect the resources from deletion by filling in the missing info
  • improve the quality of the resources to improve their ranking
  • protect the users by moderating the web chat or the IRC chat
  • improve the popularity of the platform as social managers
  • testing the platform on different devices after each upgrade

...and so on. All volunteers are equals and all tasks are equally important for the health of the free software community. All volunteers may ask for training, advice and credit.

If you'd like to participate, please introduce yourself via email or by any other mean.
